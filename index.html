Pigment Series
Model No. 9544 / 9554 / 25624
Technology
Poly Pigment Process
Pigment dyeing is normally limited to natural fibers — but this series applies an equivalent process to 100% polyester, achieving a vintage used-look never before done this way in workwear.

Eliminates all the downsides of cotton pigment dyeing: no weight, no wrinkling, no slow drying, no stiffness. All the aesthetic, none of the compromise — lightweight, quick-dry, and easy care intact.

Production includes an auxiliary agent to reduce color bleed vs. samples.
Limited Run Only Poly 100% Used Finish Lightweight
Care Notice — Important
Color Fading Info
Due to the nature of the pigment process, color fading and color transfer will occur. Treat like dark denim — wash separately, especially when new.

Color transfer to car seats, white innerwear, or light-colored fabrics is possible. Please inform customers before purchase. Not covered under warranty.

Trade show samples have stronger color bleed. Final production improved with auxiliary agent finishing.
